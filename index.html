What is PicoOS ?

PicoOS is OS specificly made for Raspberry Pi pico borads and procesors

Supported devices and chips:

Raspberry pi Pico

Raspberry pi Pico W

Raspberry pi Pico 2

Raspberry pi Pico 2 W

Developer borads with RP2040 or RP2540 Chip
